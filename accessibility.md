*Last updated: July 11, 2026*

Accessibility is core to our mission — we make language tools that are accessible to children of all abilities, and this website should be no different.

## Our commitment

We aim to conform to the [Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1, Level AA](https://www.w3.org/WAI/WCAG21/quickref/). Measures on this site include:

- **Color contrast** — text colors are checked against WCAG AA contrast ratios
- **Keyboard navigation** — every link and control is reachable and usable by keyboard, with visible focus indicators and a skip-to-content link
- **Semantic structure** — proper headings, landmarks, and navigation labels for screen readers
- **Language tagging** — Punjabi (ਪੰਜਾਬੀ) text is marked with the correct language attribute so screen readers pronounce it properly
- **Reduced motion** — animations are disabled for visitors who prefer reduced motion
- **Responsive text** — the layout works with browser zoom and larger text sizes

## Known limitations

This is a small site and we review it regularly. If anything doesn't work with your screen reader, keyboard, or other assistive technology, that's a bug we want to fix.

## Feedback

If you encounter an accessibility barrier on this site, please reach us via [www.roohmadhani.com](https://www.roohmadhani.com) and we'll do our best to fix it promptly.
