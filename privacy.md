*Last updated: July 11, 2026*

Rooh Madhani, LLC ("we," "us") makes tools for children and families, so we hold ourselves to a high bar on privacy. This policy covers this website (roohmadhani.github.io / www.roohmadhani.com) and our apps.

## What this website collects

**Nothing.** This website:

- sets **no cookies**
- runs **no analytics or tracking**
- has **no accounts, forms, or sign-ups**
- makes **no requests to third-party services**: all fonts, images, and styles are served from our own site, so your visit is not shared with advertising or font networks

## What our apps collect

**Nothing.** The same promise applies to every app we publish. Our apps:

- run **fully offline**, with no accounts and no sign-ups
- show **no ads** and contain **no in-app purchases**
- run **no analytics, crash reporting, or third-party SDKs**
- collect **no data of any kind**: progress, such as stars earned or where you left off, is saved only on your device and never leaves it

## Hosting

This site is hosted on **GitHub Pages** (GitHub, Inc.). Like virtually all web hosts, GitHub may log basic technical information about visits (such as IP addresses) for security and operational purposes. We do not receive, store, or use these logs. See [GitHub's privacy statement](https://docs.github.com/en/site-policy/privacy-policies/github-general-privacy-statement) for details.

## Children's privacy

Our work is family-focused: our apps are made for children, and this website is designed to be safe for children to browse. Because neither the site nor our apps collect personal information from anyone, they collect no personal information from children under 13 (COPPA) or under 16 (GDPR).

## Your rights

Privacy laws such as the EU's GDPR and various US state laws give you rights over personal data an organization holds about you. Because neither this website nor our apps collect personal data, there is typically nothing for us to access, correct, or delete, but if you have any question or concern, we want to hear it.
